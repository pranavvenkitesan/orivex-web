General Information.

My design approach is rooted in user-centered principles. I begin every project by understanding your goals, target audience, and brand identity. Through research and collaboration, I create wireframes and prototypes to visualize the user journey.

Project timelines depend on the scope and complexity of your requirements. Most standard websites are completed within 2-8 weeks. The process includes initial consultation, design, development, revisions, and final launch. I provide a detailed project plan with clear milestones and regular progress updates, so you always know what to expect and when.

I utilize a range of modern technologies to build high-performance websites. My core stack includes HTML5, CSS3 (with preprocessors like SASS), and JavaScript (ES6+). For dynamic and interactive experiences, I use frameworks such as React and Vue.js. For content management, I work with WordPress and headless CMS solutions.
